The internet as the front page:

Over break a friend texted me with a picture of an old New York Times they found in their grandparents’ attic: the front page announced “MEN WALK ON MOON.” I started tearing up. There’s a beauty in the finite that the internet has erased. information is too accessible (or, rather, information is hyper accessible, disinformation is too accessible). Maybe the power of editors was too great before. Deciding what stories got told meant that many stories went ignored. But having certain national stories told in national sources gave helped bring people together. Is twitter’s top trending the new front page? in some ways it feels like it, but we don’t seem to be brought together in the same way when the story is #TaylorIsOverParty
